## 3. Map data: committed to this repository (`lib/tiles/`)

Unlike a runtime-fetch deploy, the campus tiles **are committed** to this repository
so the GitHub Pages site is fully static and self-contained. They are downloaded by
`tools/fetch_tiles.mjs` from the sources below; the required attributions are shown
persistently in the app UI and reproduced here.

### Satellite imagery: `lib/tiles/img/`
> Imagery **© Esri, Maxar, Earthstar Geographics** — Esri World Imagery
> (ArcGIS `World_Imagery` map service, sub-metre).

Sub-metre imagery is needed to make the campus recognisable (EOX Sentinel-2 at
~10 m/px was too coarse). Esri World Imagery is used with attribution for this
**educational / non-commercial** documentary. Esri's terms restrict redistribution
and commercial/high-volume use; anyone reusing these tiles should review Esri's
terms of use (https://www.esri.com/en-us/legal/terms/full-master-agreement) and,
for production or commercial use, obtain a licensed basemap or an alternative source.
Source: https://server.arcgisonline.com/ArcGIS/rest/services/World_Imagery/MapServer

### Elevation: `lib/tiles/dem/` — synthetic flat baseline
The Hung Hom campus sits at/near sea level and is effectively flat, and the AWS
Terrarium DEM has no tiles above zoom 15. So at the campus zoom (z18) the DEM tiles
are **synthetic flat 0 m tiles** generated by `tools/fetch_tiles.mjs` (solid
`rgb(128,0,0)`, the Terrarium encoding of 0 m). No third-party elevation data is
redistributed for this build.
